Bone Marrow Transplant (BMT) better known as haematopoietic stem cell transplant (HSCT) as bone marrow cells as transplanted

Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a healthy donor

Medicines and in certain situations radio therapy as well is given to get rid of diseased marrow and bone marrow cells from healthy donor is given to replace it.

IN autologous bone marrow transplant, high dose chemotherapy is given to kill cancer cells and child own marrow cells are given back

What are the types of Bone Marrow Transplant?

Autologous Bone Marrow Transplant - Marrow cells are collected from the patient's own body and transfused back after high dose chemotherapy.

Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Sibling Donor) - Replacement of diseased and defected marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched sibling donor.

Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Unrelated Donor) - Replacement of diseased and defected marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched donor obtained from bone marrow registries in India and abroad.

Haplo-Identical Bone Marrow Transplant - Replacement of diseased and defected marrow with marrow from the patient.

Umbilical cord Bone Marrow Transplant - Replacement of diseased and defected marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched umbilical cord obtained from umbilical cord registries in India and abroad.

The warning signs of Immune deficiency

Failure to thrive - not gaining weight and height as per age norms.

Infections warranting multiple hospitalizations.

Requirement of intravenous antibiotics to clear infections.

2 or more episodes of pneumonia.

Family history of death of children at young age due to immune deficiency.

Repeated episodes of diarrhoea.

2 or more episodes of sinus infections within a year.

2 or more episodes of ear discharge.

Repeated skin infections.

Repeated abscess formation
(liver abscess, brain abscess).

BMT is the treatment for choice for blood disorders like Thalassemia, Sickle Cell Anaemia, bone marrow failure syndromes like Aplastic anaemia, serious immune deficiencies and certain genetic metabolic disorders.
It is also used to treat high risk cancers like some cases of Acute Lymphobiastic Leukemia, Acute Myeloid Leukemia, Multiple Myeloma and solid tumors like Neuroblastoma, Ewings Sarcoma and certain relapsed cancers like Lymphomas.
